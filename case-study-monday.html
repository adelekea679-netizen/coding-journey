Monday.com Sales & CRM Workflow

Project Overview

Designed and implemented a centralized CRM and sales management workflow using Monday.com. The system provided a clear overview of customer interactions, sales opportunities, and project progress.

Business Challenge

The organization managed leads through spreadsheets and emails, making it difficult to monitor sales activities, follow up with prospects, and generate accurate reports.

Objectives

  • Create a centralized CRM workspace.
  • Improve lead tracking.
  • Automate repetitive sales tasks.
  • Build real-time dashboards.
  • Increase team visibility.

Tools Used

  • Monday.com
  • Google Workspace
  • Slack
  • Zapier

Key Deliverables

  • Sales Pipeline
  • Lead Management Board
  • Automated Notifications
  • Performance Dashboard
  • Weekly Reports

Project Outcome

The new workflow improved visibility into the sales process, reduced manual follow-ups, and made collaboration between sales and operations more efficient.
